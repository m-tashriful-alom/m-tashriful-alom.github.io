Voce Civica: Giving Citizens a Voice Through AI

By M Tashriful Alom • May 2026 • Project Case Study

RomeCup 2026 National Winner

A "Make It Real" Innovation Project by Amazon & Fondazione Mondo Digitale

Being an active citizen shouldn't be difficult. Yet, for many living in cities like Milan, reporting a broken streetlamp or hazardous waste in a park feels like an uphill battle. Websites are hidden, forms are overwhelming, and reports often disappear into a "black hole" without updates.

This is why my team and I developed Voce Civica. Born from the Make It Real contest, Voce Civica is a civic-tech platform designed to simplify communication between citizens and their local municipalities (Comuni) using cutting-edge AI.

"Voce Civica is not just an app; it is a digital bridge. We wanted to ensure that being an active citizen is no longer a privilege for the tech-savvy, but a right accessible to everyone."

From Milan to the National Final

Our journey began with a simple observation: we noticed broken glass in the parks of Milan—a danger to children and pets. When we tried to report it, we realized even "tech-folks" like us found the official process overwhelming and non-inclusive.

We took this problem to the Make It Real initiative, organized by Fondazione Mondo Digitale and Amazon. After refining our prototype, we were invited to the Amazon Innovation Day in Novara, where our project was recognized as the best among teams from Lombardy and Piedmont.

The climax of this journey was RomeCup 2026 at Sapienza University of Rome, where Voce Civica was celebrated as a national winner for its impact and technical feasibility.

Technical Architecture & Innovation

To ensure high performance and sustainability, we built Voce Civica on a modern, serverless stack:

  • Frontend: FlutterFlow for a seamless cross-platform experience (iOS, Android, Web).
  • Backend: Python for robust AI integration and PA (Public Administration) system connectivity.
  • AI Core: We utilized RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ensure our AI assistant provides accurate, municipality-specific information.
  • AWS Ecosystem:
    • Amazon OpenSearch: For vector-based clustering to prevent duplicate reports.
    • Amazon Transcribe & Translate: Making the app inclusive through voice-to-text and multi-language support.
    • AWS Sovereign Cloud: Ensuring GDPR compliance and data privacy.

Social Impact: A Safer, More Livable City

Inclusivity Multilingual & Vocal services for all residents.
Efficiency AI-driven urgency scoring for municipalities.

For the citizens, Voce Civica offers transparency and ease of use. For the municipalities, it reduces the administrative workload by automatically clustering similar reports and prioritizing urgent safety hazards.

The Road Ahead

Launching a project within the Italian Public Administration requires a concrete strategy. Our 5-phase roadmap includes:

  1. Alignment with Designers Italia and PNRR 1.4.1 standards.
  2. Testing in the AgID Sandbox.
  3. Partnering with "in-house" entities like ARIA or Cefriel.
  4. Pilot testing through Sperimentazione Italia.
  5. National scaling via MePA and the reuse model.
