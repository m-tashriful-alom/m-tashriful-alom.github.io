Voce Civica: Giving Citizens a Voice Through AI

By M Tashriful Alom • RomeCup 2026 National Winner

RomeCup 2026 Champion

Innovation and Social Impact for the Cities of Tomorrow

Being an active citizen shouldn't be difficult. Yet, for many living in cities like Milan, reporting a broken streetlamp or hazardous waste in a park feels like an uphill battle. Websites are hidden, forms are overwhelming, and reports often disappear into a "black hole" without updates. Voce Civica was created to solve this.

The Road to Success

We took this problem to the Make It Real initiative, organized by Fondazione Mondo Digitale and Amazon. After refining our prototype, we were invited to the Amazon Innovation Day in Novara, where our project was recognized as the best among teams from Lombardy and Piedmont.

The journey culminated at RomeCup 2026, held at Sapienza University of Rome. Our project was selected as the winner for its ability to bridge the gap between residents and public administration through advanced technology.

The AI Civic Assistant: Beyond General Chatbots

The heart of Voce Civica is its AI Chat Assistant. Unlike general LLMs like ChatGPT or Gemini, which can sometimes provide generic or incorrect information, our assistant is a specialized tool for the local community.

How it works: RAG Technology

We use Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to train the AI specifically on the official data, regulations, and services of the local Comune. This ensures:

  • Accuracy: The AI only responds based on the verified library of information provided by the municipality.
  • Utility: Citizens can ask "How do I renew my parking permit?" or "Where is the nearest waste collection center?" and get instant, correct answers.
  • Guidance: The AI helps users categorize their reports and understands the next steps in the administrative process.

Technical Architecture

Voce Civica is built for scalability and security using an enterprise-grade stack:

  • Frontend: Cross-platform development via FlutterFlow.
  • Backend: Python for AI logic and API orchestration.
  • Cloud Infrastructure:
    • Amazon OpenSearch: Vector storage for RAG and automatic report clustering.
    • Amazon Transcribe & Translate: Ensuring multi-language and vocal accessibility.
    • AWS Sovereign Cloud: Keeping personal data safe within the EU.

Social Impact & Inclusivity

Our main goal is Social Impact. By offering vocal services and multilingual support, we ensure that nobody is left behind—regardless of their language or technical ability. We are building a safer, more responsive city where data privacy is a fundamental right, not an option.
